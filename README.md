Ever see those eye-catching posts on X like "Is this real?" or "AI just surpassed humanity!?"<br>

- Fact-check surprising AI claims and tech news on X
- Read and discuss AI research papers together
- Share technical insights
- Help refine X Community Notes before posting

## Who should join:
- Intermediate to advanced AI users
- Anyone curious about AI facts vs hype
- Community Note contributors
